Respect is one thing, passion is another. Get Born will showcase the skateboarding culture and lifestyle in Chicago and Montreal through online articles featuring local teams, events, shops, artists, brands, and skaters that make the culture what is is. These two underground cities are filled with homies who are working for what they believe in and doing what they love by getting involved with their local skate scene. By demonstrating this, Get Born hopes to inspire an understanding of the indescribably happiness skateboarding encourages in peoples lives and establish the importance of supporting your local skate scene. Eventually we wish to expand out site and cover different cities around the world.

We are open to people and their ideas; hit us up if you have any.
